When can you request records about another person?

To request records about a child under 12 years old (including your own child) or on behalf of another person, you must provide either:

  • Proof of your authority to act on the person’s behalf
  • That person’s signed and informed consent to receive their records

What is a record?

Records are anything on which information is recorded or stored. They can be graphic, electronic or mechanical, and may include books, documents, maps, drawings, letters, vouchers papers or other materials.
