A proceeding is any court, administrative tribunal, or formal arbitration process. The FOIPPA Policy & Procedures Manual defines proceedings as “activities governed by rules of court or rules of judicial or quasi-judicial tribunals that can result in a judgement of a court or ruling of a judicial or quasi-judicial tribunal.”
