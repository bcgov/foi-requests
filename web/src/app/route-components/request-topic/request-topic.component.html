What kinds of records are you looking for?

Are you looking for these types of records?


Police Records from a Police visit?
We do not hold these records. Your records can be requested through the RCMP or the municipal police force where the incident occurred would.

Report Cards, Individualized Education Plans and/or School Records?
We do not hold these records. Your records can be request through in the school district office of the school you were attending.

Health Records from Health Authorities?
We do not hold these records. Your records can be requested through the Health Authorities that provided care.
